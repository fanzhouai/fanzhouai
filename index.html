I'm a Post-doc at Department of Computer Science at Université Laval supervised by Prof. Mario Marchand. I'm working on the DEpendable and Explainable Learning (DEEL) project with Prof. Christian Gagné (ULaval) and Prof. Frédéric Precioso (Université Côte d’Azur, France).

I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Science also from Université Laval with Prof. Brahim Chaib-Draa and Prof. Boyu Wang in Western University. Prior to my Ph.D. program, I was working on vehicle engineering especially on electrical vehicles control at Beihang University.

My research interests includes transfer learning, domain adaptation and multi-task learning, with their applications for autonomous driving vehicles and intelligent aerocrafts.
